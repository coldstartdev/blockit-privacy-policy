BlockIt – Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

BlockIt does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data or browsing history.

All extension data — including your allowlist, custom rules, and preferences — is stored locally in your browser or in your personal Chrome sync storage, which is governed by Google's own privacy policy.

No analytics, telemetry, or tracking of any kind is included in this extension.

The only outbound network requests made by the extension are plain GET requests to download ad filter lists from easylist.to and fanboy.co.nz. No user data is included in those requests.
